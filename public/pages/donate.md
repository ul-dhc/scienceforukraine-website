# Donate

#ScienceForUkraine warmly welcomes donations that support Ukrainian scholars and students affected by Russia’s war.

We are a volunteer-run NGO. None of our team members receives payment from donations. At the moment, donations are used to support the [**#ScienceForUkraine Micro Travel Grant Programme**](/mtg), which helps early-career scholars affiliated with Ukrainian academic institutions take part in international conferences.

Every contribution is valued, regardless of the amount. Together, many small donations can make a real difference for young Ukrainian researchers who are trying to remain active in international science under wartime conditions.

## Targeted donations

We also welcome targeted donations for the Micro Travel Grant Programme.

If you would like to support Ukrainian scholars in a specific discipline, research field, conference, training school, organisation, or academic community, please contact us. Where possible, we can open a dedicated MTG call connected to your donation.

This can help Ukrainian scholars take part in a particular event or support early-career researchers working in a specific academic area.

For targeted donations, please contact us at [info@scienceforukraine.eu](mailto:info@scienceforukraine.eu).

## What do we do with the money?

In 2024, we established the **#ScienceForUkraine Micro Travel Grant Programme** for early-career scholars affiliated with Ukrainian academic institutions.

The programme helps cover travel and accommodation costs related to active participation in international conferences and other scientific events, including oral presentations, poster presentations, workshops, training schools, and research-related meetings.

By supporting this programme, you help Ukrainian scholars stay connected to international academic networks, present their research, develop collaborations, and continue their scientific work despite the war.
